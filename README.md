# Research-IT-Schools-Upsell-Strategy
Strategic analysis of IT schools to identify unique offers, learning models, and career support formats. Insights support product positioning, upsell packaging, and SEO planning for an “Analyst Turnkey” concept.

Objective

- Identify unique selling points that strengthen course value
- Compare training formats, career support, and product design
- Group offers into categories: learning, coaching, career outcomes, community
- Prepare the foundation for an upsell package ("Turnkey Analyst")
- Map insights to SEO and analytics: filters, tags, keywords
- Benchmark against our own product: what to enhance, adapt, or add
  
Categories of Unique Offers
  
- Learning tracks: Digital Marketing, Data Engineering, Data Science & AI, Digital Literacy, Product Management, Cybersecurity
- Professional simulation: Hackathons, demo days, peer reviews
- Career support: 1:1 mentoring, career tracks, soft skills, pitch training, strategic coaching
- Community features: AMA sessions, IT English Speaking Club, networking events (e.g. Elevate)
- Expert access: Dual mentorship model (industry mentor + tutor)
- Partnerships: Collaboration with global tech companies for reskilling
- Financial models: ISA (income share agreement), job placement guarantees, ESF+ support (KOMPASS), installment plans
  
Agile Learning Formats

- Sprints: Short learning cycles with clear goals (1–2 weeks)
- Scrum rituals: Planning, daily standups, retrospectives
- Team roles: Product Owner, Scrum Master, developer/analyst teams
- Demo days: Presentations to mentors and experts
- Peer review: Students evaluate each other’s work to build critical thinking and soft skills
  Upsell Package: “Turnkey Analyst”
  
A bundled offer combining:

- Structured learning path
- Career coaching and mentoring
- Industry simulation and demo days
- Community access and expert feedback
- Strategic positioning for job market entry
  
# Use Cases

This research supports:

- Product design and differentiation
- Marketing and communication strategy
- Curriculum development
- Career services and upsell packaging
- SEO and analytics planning
